Book Your Room in
4 Easy Steps

No queues, no paperwork, no stress — a fully digital flow from registration to room access.

Create Your Account

Register with your matric number, faculty, department, level, gender, and university email. Verify your email to activate your account and unlock full platform access.

1

Create Your Account

Register with your matric number, department, level, and email. Verify to activate your account.

2

Browse & Select a Room

Browse live rooms filtered by your gender automatically. Pick your preferred block, room type, and price range. Real availability from Firestore — no guessing.

Pay Your Hostel Fee

Complete payment securely via Paystack. Your room is reserved and booking confirmed automatically the instant payment is verified. No waiting for admin approval.

3

Pay Your Hostel Fee

Pay via Paystack. Room auto-reserved the instant payment is verified by the system.

4

Get Your Approval Letter

Download and print your QR-coded approval letter from your dashboard. Present it at the hostel gate — security scans the QR code to verify your booking and grant room access instantly.

Built for Everyone
in the Hostel Ecosystem

Each user type gets a tailored experience with role-specific access, tools, and dashboards.

Student

  • Browse & book rooms live
  • Pay hostel fees via Paystack
  • Download QR approval letter
  • Submit maintenance complaints
  • Register expected visitors
  • Leave verified reviews
🏠

Hostel Admin

  • Manage room inventory & pricing
  • View real-time financial reports
  • Assign & resolve complaints
  • Monitor live occupancy
  • Manage student records
  • Control hostel block settings
👮

  • Scan student QR codes at gate
  • Validate visitor passes

  • Flag unauthorized entry attempts

  • Access full maintenance history
