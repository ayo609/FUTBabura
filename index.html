Security & Verification

Only Real FUTBabura
Students Get Access

This is not an open platform. Every account is verified against official university records before any booking can be made.

🎓
Matric Number Verified
Your matric number must match the official FUTBabura format (SIT/XXX/YY/0001) and is checked for uniqueness before registration completes.
Active
📋
JAMB Number Required
Your JAMB registration number is collected and cross-verified by the admin team. Your name must match your JAMB record to be fully approved.
Active
📧
Email Verification
A verification link is sent to your email immediately after registration. You cannot book a room until your email address is confirmed.
Active
🚫
No Duplicate Accounts
One matric number, one phone number, one JAMB number, one email per student — strictly enforced. Duplicate registration attempts are blocked in real time.
Active
📱
Device Fingerprinting
Each device is fingerprinted on registration. Multiple account creation attempts from the same device are automatically flagged and blocked.
Active
🔒
Firestore Security Rules
Every database operation is protected by server-side Firestore security rules. No student can read, write or modify another student's data — ever.
Active
ℹ️
This platform is exclusively for admitted FUTBabura students. Unauthorised access attempts are logged and reported to the university's IT security team.
